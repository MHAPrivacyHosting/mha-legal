Privacy Policy
Effective Date: April 23, 2026 Last Updated: April 23, 2026

Mile High Automations LLC does not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising. SMS messages are only sent in connection with services you have requested, and you can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

1. Overview

Mile High Automations LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is a Colorado limited liability company providing AI automation and local SEO services to businesses in the Denver metropolitan area. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website (milehighautomations.com), contact us, or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Your name and business name
  • Phone number and email address
  • Business address and service area
  • Information submitted through contact forms or booking requests
  • Communications you send us via email, SMS, or phone

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website or search query

2.3 Information Collected Through Our Services

When clients use our Speed to Lead AI automation system, the following information may be collected on behalf of our clients from their customers:

  • Caller name and phone number
  • Nature of service requested
  • Property or business address
  • Call recordings and transcripts (where permitted by law and disclosed to callers)
  • SMS message content

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide our services
  • To send automated SMS notifications related to services you have requested
  • To improve our website and service offerings
  • To send service-related communications to clients
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse of our systems

4. SMS Messaging

If you or your customers receive SMS messages through our Speed to Lead automation system:

  • SMS messages are sent only in connection with services you or your business has requested
  • Every automated SMS includes opt-out instructions ("Reply STOP to opt out")
  • Replying STOP will immediately remove you from further automated messages
  • Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan
  • We do not send unsolicited marketing SMS messages
  • Message frequency varies depending on service activity

5. How We Share Your Information

5.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors who help us deliver our services. These vendors are contractually required to protect your information. Our current service providers include: Twilio Inc. (phone/SMS), Vapi AI (voice agent), Make (automation), GoHighLevel (CRM), Anthropic PBC (AI processing), and Calendly (scheduling).

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.

5.3 We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Client data is retained for the duration of the service agreement plus 12 months. Call recordings and transcripts are retained for 90 days unless otherwise required.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. To exercise any right, contact us at hello@milehighautomations.com.

9. Colorado Privacy Rights

Colorado residents have additional rights under the Colorado Privacy Act (CPA). To submit a request, email hello@milehighautomations.com with subject line "Colorado Privacy Request."

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
